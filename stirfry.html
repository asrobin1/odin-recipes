Stirfry

Introduction

Nothing makes me feel like a 5 star chef than the simplistic yet majestic art of crafting a stirfry. Okay maybe not a super chef, but I certainly enjoy the ease and taste of a good stirfry. What I love most is how much flavor it brings to the vegetables and protein. You can tailor a stirfry to whatever dietary needs you have. Just remember to cook each ingredient separately with your leafy greens and noodles dead last!
