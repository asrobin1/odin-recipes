Baked Sweet Potato

Introduction

I love sweet potatoes if it wasn't obvious. I've been cutting sugar out of my diet for a while now, and the sweet potato gives me that sweet taste without the added processed sugars. Oh and did I mention it's also healthy too? I have a pretty simple recipe for it that you can easily memorize.

Ingredients

  • Sweet potato (duh!)
  • Asian 5 Spice (the cinnamon really kicks it into high gear)
  • A dash of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Peel and chop sweet potato to your liking.
  2. Place your chopped sweet potato in a mixing bowl. Add a little bit of oil and season with Asian 5 Spice. Mix well.
  3. Place seasoned sweet potato chunks on an oil baking sheet (I prefer to use wax paper)
  4. Set your oven to 400 degrees F and bake for 20 minutes.
  5. Turn off oven and let cool for 2 minutes before serving.
