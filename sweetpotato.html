Baked Sweet Potato

Introduction

I love sweet potatoes if it wasn't obvious. I've been cutting sugar out of my diet for a while now, and the sweet potato gives me that sweet taste without the added processed sugars. Oh and did I mention it's also healthy too? I have a pretty simple recipe for it that you can easily memorize.
